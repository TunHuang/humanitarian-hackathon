Our Approach
Access to real farmers

  • We have a substantial number of farmer visits who are willing to voluntarily take time off to come to our little farms, they assist with practical work.
  • We also have speakers who come to our schools and do motivational talks

Educational resources

  • Documentaries (film)

Our Values

Children are our future

The great Nelson Mandela once said -”history will judge us by the difference we make in the lives of our children”. It is up to to us to help them make better health choices for their future.

Respect and Nurture

Teaching our kids about an in depth understanding of the relationship between nature and humans will make them nurture and respect our environment.

Farming is Cool

We’re changing the mindset of the children to grow up understanding the coolness that is behind farming. Instilling this at such a tender age could only normalise the subject of farming and ultimately change the children’s mindset.
